Our mission

To develop technology enabling autonomous robotic disassembly of lithium-ion electric vehicle batteries.

Currently EV battery packs are typically either burned or shredded whole to be recycled, we are working on technology to enable direct recycling by using autonomous robots to disassemble battery packs and recover materials with greater efficiency and lower emissions.

Leveraging machine learning and transformer neural networks, we collect and utilize sensor and vision data to enhance robot hardware with adaptive and predictive motion planning capabilities. Our software can then use this data to train custom robots to perform these dangerous and challenging tasks with adaptive intelligence with continuous learning capabilities.
