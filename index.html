Our values

We are passionate about using robotics and artificial intelligence for the good of humanity.

Be world-class.
Our robots should improve the average standard of living for all of humanity.
Take responsibility.
Technology by itself is something neutral. We have a corporate responsibility to mitigate possible negative impacts of our creations.     
Be supportive.
If we identify a problem, we have an obligation to try to also identify the solution. Collaboration helps us find solutions faster.  
Always learning.
 We learn from our mistakes, while staying curious and humble. 
Share everything you know.
Robotics is cross-disciplinary and no one person know it all. Sharing knowledge makes us all better!   
Enjoy downtime.
Improving quality of life for all humanity includes our employees! Spend time doing what you love with those you love.    

Meet our leadership

We are a dynamic team of individuals who love robotics, AI and startups and are dedicated to delivering the best results for our clients.

  • Joseph Cole, PhD

    Founder / CEO

    Joe has 24 years experience developing vision and machine learning algorithms across a variety of industries, including defense, oil & gas, semiconductors, and medical devices. He has a PhD in Applied Physics from Rice University, and served as an officer in the US Army Reserve for 22 years.

  • Duncan Miller, MBA

    Co-founder / CFO

    Duncan has 20 years of experience in startups, with extensive experience in finance, operations and software engineering. He has expertise in large language models, prompt engineering and test-driven AI development. Duncan earned an MBA in Entrepreneurship from Babson College and had his first successful startup exit in 2023.
